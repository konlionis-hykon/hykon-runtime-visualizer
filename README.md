Hykon Runtime Visualizer

Interactive demonstration of runtime conversational governance for large language models.

This repository contains a live browser-based visualiser illustrating the Hykon conversational governance overlay, a system designed to manage epistemic risk and reasoning stability at inference time without modifying model weights.

Overview

Modern large language models are highly capable but tend to generate fluent answers even under uncertainty. This creates risk in domains such as:

scientific reasoning
policy and governance
medical and legal contexts
research synthesis
autonomous systems

Hykon introduces a structured runtime layer that:

evaluates conversational conditions
enforces stability constraints
routes reasoning through bounded recursive refinement
halts generation when epistemic risk exceeds thresholds

This visualiser demonstrates these mechanisms in an interactive environment.
Status

This is an early research prototype (v4.2 demonstration).
The focus is clarity and experimentation rather than optimisation.

Feedback and testing are encouraged.
